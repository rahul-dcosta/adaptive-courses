### VAT (Value Added Tax)
**Rate:** 5%

**Registration required if:**
- Revenue >AED 375,000 (~$102K/year) - Mandatory
- Revenue >AED 187,500 (~$51K/year) - Voluntary

**For you:**
- Start without VAT registration (under threshold)
- Register when you hit $100K/year revenue
- Charge 5% VAT to UAE customers (if registered)
- Claim back VAT on expenses

**International customers:**
- 0% VAT (export of services)
- Most of your revenue will be international â†’ likely 0% VAT

---

### Personal Income Tax

No personal income tax in UAE. All revenue you take as salary/dividends is tax-free.
