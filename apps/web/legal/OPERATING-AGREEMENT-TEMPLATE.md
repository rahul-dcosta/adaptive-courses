### ARTICLE III: MANAGEMENT STRUCTURE

**3.1 Manager-Managed LLC**

**THIS IS A MANAGER-MANAGED LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY.** The business and affairs of the Company shall be managed exclusively by the Managing Member.

**3.2 Managing Member**

**[US CITIZEN CO-FOUNDER NAME]** is hereby designated as the sole Managing Member and shall have full and exclusive authority to manage the business and affairs of the Company.

**3.3 Powers of Managing Member**

The Managing Member shall have the sole and exclusive authority to:

a) **Day-to-Day Operations:**
   - Make all business decisions
   - Manage daily operations
   - Hire, fire, and compensate employees and contractors
   - Set operational policies and procedures

b) **Contracts and Agreements:**
   - Execute contracts, agreements, and instruments on behalf of the Company
   - Sign checks, drafts, and other financial documents
   - Open and manage bank accounts
   - Apply for loans or credit lines

c) **Financial Decisions:**
   - Make expenditures and investments
   - Set pricing for products and services
   - Approve budgets and financial plans
   - File tax returns and manage accounting

d) **Business Development:**
   - Develop and launch products
   - Enter into partnerships or collaborations
   - Acquire assets or businesses
   - Make strategic decisions

e) **Legal and Administrative:**
   - Represent the Company in legal matters
   - Maintain Company records and licenses
   - Ensure regulatory compliance
   - Amend this Operating Agreement (with member approval for material changes)

**3.4 PASSIVE MEMBER RESTRICTIONS - H1B COMPLIANCE**

**RAHUL D'COSTA IS A PASSIVE MEMBER WITH NO MANAGEMENT AUTHORITY.**

Rahul D'Costa **shall NOT:**

a) **Participate in Day-to-Day Operations**
   - Shall not manage, direct, or supervise business activities
   - Shall not make operational decisions
   - Shall not represent himself as an officer or employee

b) **Execute Contracts**
   - Shall not sign agreements on behalf of the Company
   - Shall not commit the Company to obligations
   - Shall not negotiate terms with vendors, customers, or partners

c) **Hire or Manage Personnel**
   - Shall not hire, fire, or supervise employees
   - Shall not direct the work of contractors
   - Shall not set compensation or benefits

d) **Handle Financial Operations**
   - Shall not sign checks or wire transfers
   - Shall not approve expenditures (except as allowed in Section 3.5)
   - Shall not manage bank accounts or credit lines

e) **Publicly Represent the Company**
   - Shall not hold himself out as an officer or manager
   - Shall not make public statements on behalf of the Company
   - Shall not conduct business development

**IMMIGRATION COMPLIANCE:** Rahul D'Costa acknowledges that participation in management or operational activities would violate his H1B visa status. Any violation of these restrictions may result in immediate termination of his membership interest.

**3.5 Rights of Passive Member (Rahul D'Costa)**

Notwithstanding the restrictions in Section 3.4, Rahul D'Costa retains the following limited rights:

a) **Financial Rights:**
   - Receive profit distributions in accordance with his membership percentage
   - Review quarterly financial statements
   - Receive annual tax documents (K-1)

b) **Information Rights:**
   - Inspect Company books and records (upon reasonable notice)
   - Receive annual financial reports
   - Be informed of major Company events

c) **Voting Rights on Major Decisions:**
   - Approval required for membership transfers or admissions
   - Approval required for amendments to this Operating Agreement
   - Approval required for sale or dissolution of the Company
   - Approval required for capital calls exceeding $[AMOUNT]

d) **Passive Investment:**
   - Provide capital contributions (if voluntary)
   - Discuss business strategy in advisory capacity (non-binding)
   - Introduce potential customers or partners (without negotiating terms)

**CLARIFICATION:** These rights do NOT constitute management authority. Rahul D'Costa's role is limited to that of a passive investor.

### ARTICLE IV: PROFITS, LOSSES, AND DISTRIBUTIONS

**4.1 Allocation of Profits and Losses**

Profits and losses shall be allocated to Members in proportion to their Membership Interests:

- [US CITIZEN CO-FOUNDER]: [5-10]%
- RAHUL D'COSTA: [90-95]%

**4.2 Distributions**

The Managing Member may authorize distributions at any time, in their sole discretion.

Distributions shall be made in proportion to Membership Interests.

**4.3 Tax Distributions**

The Managing Member shall authorize distributions sufficient to cover each Member's estimated tax liability on Company income ("Tax Distributions").

Tax Distributions shall be made quarterly and shall be based on the highest marginal tax rate applicable to any Member.

**4.4 Withholding**

The Company shall withhold any taxes required by law from distributions and shall remit such amounts to the appropriate tax authorities.

---

### ARTICLE V: TRANSFER OF MEMBERSHIP INTERESTS

**5.1 Restrictions on Transfer**

No Member may transfer, sell, assign, or encumber their Membership Interest without the prior written consent of all other Members.

**5.2 Right of First Refusal**

If a Member wishes to transfer their Interest:

a) They must notify the other Members in writing
b) Other Members have 30 days to purchase the Interest at the offered price
c) If not exercised, the selling Member may transfer to the third party
d) Transfer must close within 60 days or rights reset

**5.3 Drag-Along Rights**

If Members holding [75]% or more of the Membership Interests agree to sell the Company, all Members must participate on the same terms.

**5.4 Tag-Along Rights**

If a Member proposes to sell their Interest to a third party, other Members have the right to include their Interests in the sale on the same terms.

---

### ARTICLE VI: MEMBER REMOVAL AND DISSOLUTION

**6.1 Removal of Passive Member (H1B Compliance)**

**CRITICAL FOR H1B COMPLIANCE:** Rahul D'Costa must be removable without his consent.

The Managing Member may remove Rahul D'Costa as a Passive Member (and forfeit his Membership Interest) upon:

a) **Violation of H1B Restrictions:** If Rahul participates in management or operations in violation of Section 3.4
b) **Breach of Agreement:** Material breach of this Operating Agreement
c) **Illegal Activity:** Fraud, embezzlement, or criminal conduct
d) **Bankruptcy:** Filing for bankruptcy or insolvency
e) **Managing Member Discretion:** At the Managing Member's sole discretion with 90 days' written notice

**Fair Market Value Payment:** Upon removal (except for cause), Rahul D'Costa shall receive the fair market value of his Membership Interest, determined by independent valuation, payable over [12-24] months.

**6.2 Removal of Managing Member**

The Managing Member may be removed only:

a) By unanimous consent of all other Members (if multiple Members exist)
b) Upon death, incapacity, or resignation
c) Material breach of fiduciary duties

**6.3 Dissolution Events**

The Company shall dissolve upon:

a) Unanimous consent of all Members
b) Sale of all Company assets
c) Occurrence of any event requiring dissolution under Delaware law
d) Termination of the last remaining Member

**6.4 Winding Up**

Upon dissolution:

1. The Managing Member (or liquidator) shall wind up affairs
2. Assets shall be liquidated
3. Proceeds shall be distributed in the following order:
   - Creditors (including Member loans)
   - Members' capital accounts
   - Members in proportion to Membership Interests
