## FAQs

**Q: How is this different from ChatGPT?**  
A: ChatGPT gives you text. We give you structured courses with modules, lessons, quizzes, and a learning path. Plus, we ask the RIGHT questions to understand your context.

**Q: Can I request refunds?**  
A: Yes! If you're not happy with the course, email us within 24 hours for a full refund.

**Q: What topics can I learn?**  
A: Anything! Manufacturing, supply chain, nuclear energy, sales frameworks, you name it.

**Q: How long are the courses?**  
A: Depends on your timeline selection. "Tomorrow" courses are shorter and more tactical. "No rush" courses go deeper.

**Q: Do you save my data?**  
A: We save your email and generated courses. We don't sell your data or spam you.

**Q: Will you add subscriptions?**  
A: Not planning to. We like the "pay per course" model. Keeps it simple.

**Q: Can I share courses?**  
A: You can share the PDF! We might add a "public course library" later.
