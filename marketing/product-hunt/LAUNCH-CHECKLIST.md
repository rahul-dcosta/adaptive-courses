# Product Hunt Launch Checklist

## Pre-Launch (2 Weeks Before)

### Week -2: Foundation
- [ ] **Product Hunt account setup**
  - Create/update maker profile
  - Add profile photo, bio, links
  - Connect Twitter, LinkedIn
  
- [ ] **Build hunter/supporter list**
  - Identify 10-20 potential early upvoters
  - Reach out to indie hacker community
  - Engage with AI/education PH posts
  
- [ ] **Prepare assets**
  - Product logo (240x240px)
  - Cover image (1270x760px)
  - Product screenshots (5-7 images)
  - Demo GIF/video (under 2 min)
  - Thumbnail for video (1200x630px)

- [ ] **Product readiness**
  - Landing page live and polished
  - Payment flow tested
  - Analytics tracking set up
  - Error monitoring (Sentry/etc.)
  - Support email ready

---

### Week -1: Content & Strategy

- [ ] **Write Product Hunt copy**
  - Tagline (max 60 chars): "AI crash courses generated in 60 seconds for urgent learning"
  - Description (260 chars): "Need to prep for a meeting, interview, or presentation tomorrow? Generate a personalized 30-60 min crash course on any topic. No fluff, just what you need to sound intelligent."
  - First comment (detailed story - see template below)
  - Gallery descriptions for each image
  
- [ ] **Prepare launch content**
  - Twitter announcement thread
  - LinkedIn post
  - Indie Hackers post
  - Reddit posts (r/SideProject, r/entrepreneur)
  - Email to friends/network
  
- [ ] **Identify hunter (optional)**
  - Reach out to established PH hunters
  - Or self-hunt (increasingly common)
  
- [ ] **Set launch date & time**
  - Best day: **Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday**
  - Avoid: Monday (crowded), Friday (low engagement), weekends
  - Launch time: **12:01 AM PST** (Product Hunt day starts then)

## Launch Day: Hour-by-Hour

### 12:00 AM PST (Launch Moment)
- [ ] Post product to Product Hunt

## First Comment Template (Your Story)

**Title:** "I built this because I kept Googling 'learn X fast' and found nothing"

**Structure:**
1. **The Problem (Personal Story)**
   - "Two months ago, I had a client meeting about blockchain in 8 hours and knew nothing about crypto."
   - "I Googled 'blockchain crash course' and found 40-hour Udemy courses."
   - "I needed 1 hour of learning, not 40."

2. **The Insight**
   - "Realized there's a gap: just-in-time learning doesn't exist."
   - "Before job interviews, client meetings, presentations - we need speed, not depth."

3. **The Solution**
   - "So I built Adaptive Courses: type in what you need to learn, why, and when."
   - "In 60 seconds, you get a personalized 30-60 min crash course."
   - "No fluff, no 40-hour commitment - just the essentials."

4. **How It Works**
   - "Enter topic: e.g., 'blockchain basics for client meeting' or 'React fundamentals for job interview'"
   - "AI generates a custom course: modules, flashcards, actionable takeaways"
   - "Learn in 30-60 min, walk into your meeting confident"

5. **Who It's For**
   - "Consultants prepping for client meetings"
   - "Job seekers brushing up before interviews"
   - "Presenters learning a new topic overnight"
   - "Anyone who needs to sound intelligent fast"

6. **What's Next**
   - "This is v1. I'd love feedback on:"
   - "- What topics would you use this for?"
   - "- What's missing?"
   - "- Would you pay $7 for this?"

7. **Ask**
   - "Try it out and let me know what you think!"
   - "Any feedback is gold at this stage. ðŸ™"

## Best Days/Times to Launch

### Best Days (Data-Backed)
1. **Tuesday** - Highest engagement, less crowded than Monday
2. **Wednesday** - Strong engagement, good visibility
3. **Thursday** - Decent engagement, less competition

### Avoid
- **Monday** - Most crowded (everyone launches Monday)
- **Friday** - Low engagement (weekend mode)
- **Weekends** - Very low traffic

### Launch Time
- **12:01 AM PST** - This is when the PH "day" starts
- Launching early gives you 24 hours to climb the leaderboard
- Most upvotes happen between 6 AM - 6 PM PST

### Seasonal Considerations
- **Avoid:** Major holidays, summer vacation weeks, December 20-Jan 5
- **Best:** January (New Year momentum), September (back-to-school)
