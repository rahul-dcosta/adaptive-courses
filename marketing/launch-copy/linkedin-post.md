---

We built Adaptive Courses to address this. Here's how it works:

**1. Context-first personalization**

Instead of just asking "What's your skill level?" we ask:
- What's your goal? (job interview, client meeting, career development)
- What's your timeline? (30 minutes vs. 1 week)
- What's your situation? (factory tour, strategy presentation, technical implementation)

Using Claude (Anthropic's latest model), we generate 2-module courses tailored to:
- Your prior knowledge
- Your learning style (visual, hands-on, theory-first)
- Your immediate application (real examples from your domain)

**3. Efficient delivery**

- 30-90 minute read time (not 40 hours)
- Visual diagrams for complex topics
- Practical takeaways, not just theory
- Content you own forever (no subscription lock-in)

**POSTING STRATEGY:**

**When to post:**
- Best times: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-9am ET or 12-1pm ET
- Avoid: Monday (inbox overload), Friday (people check out), weekends

**Engagement:**
- Respond to every comment within 2 hours
- Ask follow-up questions (drive conversation)
- Tag relevant people if they comment (keeps thread alive)
- Share insights, not sales pitches in comments

**Profile optimization (before posting):**
- Headline: "Building adaptive learning solutions" (not "Founder at Adaptive Courses" - H1B)
- About section: Mention the company, not role
- Featured: Link to adaptivecourses.com

**What NOT to do:**
- Don't use excessive emojis (max 1-2)
- Don't say "I'm so excited to share..."
- Don't ask people to "check out my startup"
- Don't use #StartupLife or #Entrepreneurship (cringe)
- Don't mention founder title (H1B compliance)

**Professional but not corporate:**
- Use data and insights
- Share real use cases
- Invite critical feedback
- Avoid hype language ("revolutionary", "game-changing", "disrupting")
- Be helpful, not salesy
